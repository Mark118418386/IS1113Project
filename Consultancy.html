Consultancy

What is Consultancy?

Consultancy is a professional practice that gives expert advice within a particular field.

Why I think I am suited to consultancy?

To be a consultant, you must be confident and have the ability to gain trust from the customer. You must be able to chat your way to the sale. Not only that, but also a fantastic understanding of the certain area they are consulting about. I feel I would be determined enough to gain this knowledge and pass it on to future customers. I also like the aspect of possibly getting paid by commission which enhances the incentive.
