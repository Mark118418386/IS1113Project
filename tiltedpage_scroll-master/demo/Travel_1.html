Travel

Travelling is about much more, challenging yourself and finding something out of the ordinary for you and embracing it. You will discover how resourceful you really can be when exposed to new places, people and experiences. I have many places I plan on visiting including;

My favourite Country

My favourite country I have every visited has to Spain. It's a beautiful country, I could possibly see myself taking a year to live there and learn the language. I have visited several cities in Spain, however i do not feel i have yet understood the Spanish culture. I hope to take in their culture in the future and really understand where they come from and how their living conditions differ from that of Ireland.
